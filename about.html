About Us

We started KickStart with community at its core, inspired by the strength and support of our local networks in North Carolina. From the beginning, we believed that meaningful change starts with us, that to see the change we hope for in the world, we must become it. Our mission extends beyond borders, reaching vulnerable communities across the globe. By taking initiative and working hand-in-hand with our community, we aim to carry out charitable acts that uplift and impact as many lives as possible.

Our Mission

We started KickStart with one simple goal: to create meaningful change we can be proud of. This mission drives everything we do. With our community in mind, we strive to demonstrate the positive impact that’s possible through charitable acts and volunteer work. Creating change isn’t easy but with a strong, supportive community behind us, it becomes not only possible, but powerful.
